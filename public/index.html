About the Syrian Christian Congress

The Syrian Christian Congress is an institutional civic framework bringing together Syrian Christians in Syria and across the diaspora to articulate a clear and responsible national presence in Syrian public life. The Congress is grounded in the conviction that Syrian Christians are an integral part of Syria's historical, social, and cultural fabric, and partners in building a modern state based on equal citizenship, the rule of law, and the protection of human dignity for all Syrians.

Our Mission & Vision

An Authentic National Presence

We work to affirm Syrian Christian presence as an integral part of Syria's identity, and to affirm Syrian Christians as active partners in its political, social, and cultural future.

Equal Citizenship and the Rule of Law

We advocate for a unified civil state based on equal citizenship, state neutrality toward religious affiliations, judicial independence, and the protection of fundamental rights and freedoms.

Responsible Institutional Organization

We seek to transform the Syrian Christian presence in Syria and the diaspora into an organized, transparent, and representative force guided by a shared national responsibility.
